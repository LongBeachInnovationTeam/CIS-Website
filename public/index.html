The City of Long Beach is a national leader in civic, social, and government innovation. This day-long summit, sponsored by the City of Long Beach, is an opportunity for thought leaders, community members, planners, and professional staff to recognize outstanding efforts and cutting-edge innovation in the public sector.

How much does it cost?

There is no cost to participate but space is limited. Registration will be closed on October 3rd @ 5:00 p.m., or when participation has reached its capacity at 250. Parking at the Convention Center is $10.

Who should attend?

Government employees

Local government professionals, managers, and City staff.

City Officials

City commissioners, City Council members and staff.

Universities

University faculty and students interested in public administration, public policy, design thinking, government technology, business administration, and innovation.

Community

Community members interested in civic and social innovation

Businesses

Entrepreneurs, business owners, and industry leaders interested in ways to partner with local government to make life better for communities.
